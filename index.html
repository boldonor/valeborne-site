The Four Pillars

A complete framework for genomic deal risk.

Every ValeBorne audit covers the four domains that decide whether a genomic acquisition creates or destroys value.

Legal & Compliance

Consent provenance, HIPAA / GDPR exposure, data transfer rights, and IP chain-of-title across every dataset.

Technical & Data Integrity

Pipeline reproducibility, reference build consistency, batch effects, and the real defensibility of proprietary models.

Financial Health

Revenue quality, burn-vs-runway under realistic compute costs, and the unit economics of sequencing operations.

Operational Risk

Key-person dependency, vendor concentration, and integration friction with your existing data and clinical stack.
