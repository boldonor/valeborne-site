The ValeBorne Difference

Why ValeBorne?

Traditional due diligence is slow, expensive, and often misses the risks that matter most. ValeBorne combines AI-powered analysis with a proven 4-Pillar Framework to deliver comprehensive, actionable intelligence in days.

Pillar 1

Technical & Data Integrity

Reproducibility of scientific claims, platform robustness, data provenance, and technical debt.

Pillar 2

Financial Health & Sustainability

Cash runway, revenue quality, burn rate, valuation, and capital structure.

Pillar 3

Operational Risk & Scalability

Management completeness, key person dependency, cultural health, and operational maturity.

Pillar 4

Legal & Compliance

Patent portfolios, freedom-to-operate, litigation history, and IP ownership clarity.
